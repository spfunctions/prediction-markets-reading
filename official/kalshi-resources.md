## Key Concepts
- **Contract Types**: Binary YES/NO contracts on real-world events
- **Settlement**: All contracts settle at $0 or $1, resolved by objective sources (BLS, NOAA, official election results)
- **Fee Structure**: No trading fees on Kalshi as of 2025
- **Hours**: Most markets trade 24/7 except maintenance windows
- **Regulation**: CFTC-regulated Designated Contract Market (DCM)
