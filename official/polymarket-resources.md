## Key Concepts
- **Market Structure**: CLOB (Central Limit Order Book) on Polygon
- **Settlement**: UMA Optimistic Oracle with dispute resolution
- **Token Standard**: CTF (Conditional Token Framework) ERC-1155 tokens
- **Currency**: USDC on Polygon
- **Fees**: No maker fees, taker fees vary by market
- **Liquidity Rewards**: Monthly rewards program for market makers
