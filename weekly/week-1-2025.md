## Desk Breakdown

### Macro & Rates Desk 🟢

The market is overwhelmingly pricing in a 'soft landing' scenario for 2025, with a recession deemed highly unlikely (1%). Fed rate cut bets are subdued, and Powell's job security is perceived as solid, signaling strong confidence in the current policy trajectory.

### Crypto Markets & Regulation Desk ⚪

Massive speculation on Bitcoin's price range dominated volume, but conviction on extreme highs is low. Significant volume on the downside bucket suggests traders are seriously hedging a potential low. The 11% probability for $100k Bitcoin by year-end reflects cautious optimism rather than euphoria.

### Elections Desk ⚪

The sole active market, 'Donald Trump out this year?', traded with a staggering $9.76M in volume, precisely balanced at 50/50. This immense liquidity and binary uncertainty indicate the market views a major political shift as a central risk for 2025, attracting equal parts speculation and hedging.

### Geopolitics & Policy Desks ⚪

AI Regulation and the Ukraine-Russia Conflict are key active events, though their volume did not crack the top 10 this week. The focus remains on potential binary shocks rather than incremental developments in these ongoing narratives.
